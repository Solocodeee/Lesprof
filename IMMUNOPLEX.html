INDICATIONS
• Increase milk production in dairy animals.
• Effective growth promoter: long lasting anabolic and growth enhancer, increasing muscle endurance and strength.
• Ideal supportive therapy in case of:
✓ Prevention and treatment of anemia in poor growing animals or animals with debilitating type of diseases that produce weight loss.
✓ Pre-and post-anthelmintic or antibiotic treatments.
✓ Stress or during convalescence.
✓ Immune-Booster: strengthen the immune system.
SPECIAL PRECAUTIONS
✓ Do not administer to animals with hypersensitivity to any of the active ingredients of this product.
✓ Keep in a cool and dry place, protected from direct sunlight.
✓ Store at 15°-30°C.
